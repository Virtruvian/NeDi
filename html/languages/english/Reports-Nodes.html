Node Reports

Introduction

Reports focused entirely on the computers connected to your network.

Usage

  • Filter on device based criterion, if desired (won't be taken into account for links to other modules).
  • Select (multiple) reports you wish to look at.
  • Increase the limit to see more results.
  • Check 'Alternative' to sort keys rather than values.

Reports

  1. The network population at a glance.
  2. Interface statistics detect hubs or virtual machines on a managed interface, show top interface changes (e.g. travelling notebooks) and the NeDi's internal metric statistics.
  3. Empty vlans can be identifyied and eventually removed, if not needed on particular devices.
  4. IP and IF changes multiplied yield NeDi's nomad factor, an indicator for those who seem to travel a lot.
  5. Node update hisory can reveal major changes or problems in your network.
